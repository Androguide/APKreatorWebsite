Step n°1: Pick your Features

YouTube

This feature allows users to browse through your YouTube channel & watch your videos directly from your app, with no login required.

Google+

This feature allows users to browse through all your Google+ posts and to read them directly from your application, with no login required.

Twitter

This feature allows users to browse through all your tweets and read them directly from your application. (This feature is not available yet).

Step n°2: Configure your App

App Name

This defines the name of your app, which will appear in the user's app drawer and in the action-bar of your application

Package Name

The unique identifier of your app. Use a reverse-url scheme with at least 3 parts: country.company.appname

Color Scheme

Defines the main color of your app, which will be found in the action-bar, on text titles, on touch feedback, etc…

Presentation

Write a couple sentences to present yourself and/or your application

Icon

The icon of your app, which will be visible in the user's app drawer and in the action-bar of your application

Website

If you have a website, please input its url below.
A link to your website will be added in the sliding drawer of your application.
